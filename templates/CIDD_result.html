How accurate is your disease detection model?

Our model has achieved an accuracy of 99.76% on our test dataset. We are constantly working to improve its performance.

What technology is used for crop identification and disease detection?

We utilize a deep learning model based on the ResNet50 architecture, trained on a large dataset of crop and disease images.

Can I use this for identifying diseases in my garden/farm?

Yes, our tool is designed to assist farmers, gardeners, and researchers in identifying crop diseases.

Can I save or download the analysis results?

Yes, you can download the results as a PDF or text file.

Is my uploaded image stored on your servers?

We do not store your uploaded images. They are processed and deleted immediately after analysis.
